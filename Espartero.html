The Power of Office Administration

Major: Bachelor of Science in Office Administration (BSOA)

Core Topics

Office Administration is the management of daily office operations to ensure efficiency, organization, and productivity in a workplace. It involves handling administrative tasks such as communication, record keeping, scheduling, document management, customer service, office technology, and coordination of resources.

Why It Matters

Office Administration is important because it helps organizations operate smoothly and efficiently. Administrative professionals coordinate communication, manage records, organize schedules, and support daily business activities.

Career Opportunities

Office Administration offers career opportunities such as administrative assistant, office clerk, executive assistant, office administrator, records officer, receptionist, data entry specialist, customer service representative, administrative coordinator, and office manager.
